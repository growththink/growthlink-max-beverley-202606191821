FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Growthlink for?+
Growthlink is built for service-based businesses — both B2C and B2B — that want to install predictable client acquisition systems and scale without the chaos of doing everything manually.
What does the strategy call cover?+
We'll audit your current systems, identify your biggest growth bottlenecks, and map out a custom plan including CRM setup, lead nurture automation, onboarding workflows, and scaling strategy.
What tools do you use?+
We primarily build on GoHighLevel for CRM and automation, with integrations into Discord for team communication, Calendly for scheduling, and Stripe for payment processing.
Are there additional costs beyond the program?+
Yes — you should budget for ad spend (minimum ~$750/month recommended), a GoHighLevel subscription ($97/month), and domain costs. We'll walk through everything on your strategy call.
Do you offer payment plans?+
Yes. We offer flexible payment structures for both our B2C and B2B programs, including split payments and financing options through partners like Klarna, Sezzle, and PayPal. Details are discussed on your strategy call.
How quickly can I expect results?+
Timelines vary based on your business, niche, and commitment. We focus on building sustainable systems — not making unrealistic promises. Most clients see meaningful pipeline activity within the first few weeks of launch.
