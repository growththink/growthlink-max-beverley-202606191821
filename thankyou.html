FAQ

Common Questions

What if I need to reschedule?+
No problem — use the link in your confirmation email to reschedule. We just ask for at least 24 hours notice so we can offer the slot to someone else.
How should I prepare for the call?+
Know your current monthly revenue, your biggest bottleneck, and your 90-day goal. We'll also send you a brief pre-call questionnaire and relevant case studies to review.
Will I be sold on the call?+
The call is a genuine strategy session. We'll give you a clear roadmap and actionable insights. If there's a fit to work together, we'll discuss it — but there's zero pressure.
What happens after the strategy call?+
If we're a good fit, we'll walk you through our program options, payment plans, and onboarding process. If not, you'll still walk away with a clear growth plan you can implement on your own.
